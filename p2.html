Product description

VELFAC Ribo Alu 3-lags is presented as a window solution intended for projects where a narrow visual frame, durable external finish, and strong energy performance matter. The aluminium-clad exterior supports weather resistance and lower maintenance on the outside, while the overall configuration is positioned toward modern building envelopes that require improved thermal performance through triple glazing.
